In the knockout stage of the Champions League against Olympique Lyonnais on 4 March, Ronaldo scored the decisive goal in the second leg which helped United advance 2–1 on aggregate, and in the Champions League quarter final, he played as a striker and scored against Roma with a header, Despite him missing a penalty in the semi-final first leg against Barcelona, United eventually advanced to the 2007–08 Champions League final via a Paul Scholes goal.
